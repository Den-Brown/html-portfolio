The Harry Potter Movies Not Ranked

Harry Potter Films in Order

Harry Potter and the Philosophers Stone.

Harry escapes the cupboard under the stairs, goes to a new school and makes friends and enemies.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets.

Harry and his friends go monster and secret hunting, much to the delight of Tom Riddle, whom is a living memory in control of a massive snake.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban.

Harry and his friends sort of go monster and secret hunting again! This time though he meets his Godfather, who was mistaken for a monster or Grim and he also meets an actual monster (werewolf) who turned out to be a lovely, chocolate chomping teacher.
